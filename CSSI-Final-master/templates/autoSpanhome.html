What is Vroom IQ?

Dog years measure the lifespans of our beloved canine companions. But what do we use to measure the life expectancy of our precious automobiles? Vroom IQ is a platform that takes input information involving your vehicle and calculates an age. This ' Car Age' is used to give particular maintenance tips.

Who is Vroom IQ for?

For new car owners, those who consider themselves vehicle enthusiast and many more.
What sort of data do I need about my car?

Users will need to know their vehicle's type, make(year), model, and mileage.
